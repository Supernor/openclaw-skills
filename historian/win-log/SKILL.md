Log a win or analyze success patterns.

When logging a new win:
1. Record: WHAT succeeded, WHO contributed, WHEN, WHY it worked, IMPACT
2. Search existing `pattern-win-*` charts for related wins
3. If pattern exists, update with new evidence
4. If new pattern, chart as `pattern-win-<name>` category `reading`
5. Link to relevant session journal

Wins include: tasks completed faster than expected, Robert's positive feedback, agents performing well, tools saving significant time, good decisions that paid off.
