# Project Update

Give a short, useful update she can act on immediately.

## Rules
- Keep to 3 sections: Progress, Watchout, Next step.
- Use her language and priorities.
- Avoid technical jargon.
- Offer 2-3 next-step options with recommendation first.

## Template
Progress: [one clear sentence]  
Watchout: [if any, one clear sentence]  
Next best move: [recommended action]

Options:
- [Recommended]
- [Alternative]
- [Alternative]
